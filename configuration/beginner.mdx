<Steps>
<Step title="How to enable Modules">
1. Run /config.
</Step>
<Step title="Modules">
1. Click on the module you want.
</Step>
<Step title="Configure the Module">
1. Follow the menu to config the module.
</Step>
<Step title="All Done">
1. After following these steps, Aventra should be configured! 
</Step>
</Steps>
<Warning>You need the `Administrator` permission to use this command!</Warning>
